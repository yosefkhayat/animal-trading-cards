St. Bernard Dog

This dog is one of the biggest dog in the world!

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average size: between 64 and 120 kg
  • Habitat: All over the world
  • Conservation status: thriving

The St. Bernard is a breed of very large working dog from the western Alps. They were originally bred for rescue at the Great St Bernard Pass,and they has become famous through tales of alpine rescues, as well as for its enormous size.

Akita Dog

This dog is commonly raised to gard properties!

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average size: between 32 and 39 kg
  • Habitat: lived with their owners
  • Conservation status: thriving

The Akita is a large breed of dog originating from the mountainous northern regions of Japan. The Akita has a short double-coat similar to that of many other northern spitz breeds such as the Siberian Husky.

Siberian Husky Dog

This dog is one of the most desired dog in the world!

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average size: between 20 and 27 kg
  • Habitat: All over the world
  • Conservation status: thriving

The Siberian Husky is a medium size working dog breed that originated in north-eastern Siberia, Russia. It is recognizable by its thickly furred double coat, erect triangular ears, and distinctive markings.
